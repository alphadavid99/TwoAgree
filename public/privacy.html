TwoAgree — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 20 July 2026

Who we are

TwoAgree is a faith-based couples compatibility app operated by David Victor Ltd, a private limited company registered in England and Wales, company number 10437889. For UK GDPR purposes, David Victor Ltd is the data controller. You can reach us at hello@twoagree.app.

What we collect

  • Account data: email address, a display name, an optional short bio, and an optional profile photo.
  • Special-category data: your answers to questions about religious and philosophical beliefs, sex life, and health. These are treated as special-category data under UK GDPR Article 9 and are the core of what the app exists to surface.
  • Session data: the sessions you create or join, and your answers and predictions within them, shared only with the one partner in that session.
  • Consent record: the fact that you gave consent, and when. See “Retention” below — this record outlives your account.

Why we collect it (lawful basis)

We process this data to provide the alignment features you ask for. For special-category data our lawful basis is your explicit consent under Article 9(2)(a), given as a separate, unbundled tick box before you answer anything.

You can withdraw your consent at any time by deleting your account in Profile → “Delete my account”, or by emailing us. Withdrawing consent does not affect processing we carried out before you withdrew it.

Who can see your answers

Your answers in a session are visible to you and the single partner in that session, and only after you have both finished a level. They are not public and are not sold. We do not use your answers for advertising.

Your private intake answers for the Path are visible to you alone — your partner cannot read them. They are combined into a shared journey only by our server, which never exposes one partner's intake to the other.

We use Google Firebase (Authentication, Realtime Database, Hosting, Cloud Functions) as our data processor; infrastructure is hosted in the europe-west1 region.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to:

  • Access & portability: export all your data as JSON from Profile → “Export my data”. The export includes your profile, your answers, your private Path intake, and your consent record.
  • Erasure: delete your account from Profile → “Delete my account”. This removes your profile, your private intake, your login, and your contributions from every session — and deletes the session entirely if you were the only member left.
  • Rectification: edit your name, bio, and photo any time in Profile. Note that answers cannot be edited once submitted, by design — if you need an answer corrected or removed, email us.
  • Restriction and objection: ask us to pause or stop certain processing — email us.
  • Complain: you may complain to the UK Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk. We would appreciate the chance to put things right first.

To exercise any of these, email hello@twoagree.app. We aim to respond within one month, as UK GDPR requires.

Retention

We keep your account data and answers until you delete your account, at which point they are removed as described above.

One exception: we retain your consent record — the fact that you consented and the date — after your account is deleted. We keep it because it is our evidence that we were permitted to hold your data in the first place, which UK GDPR requires us to be able to demonstrate. It contains no answers and no special-category data. You can see this record at any time via “Export my data”.

Deleted data may persist in routine encrypted backups for a short period before those backups age out.

Children

TwoAgree is for adults aged 18 or over and is not directed at children. If you believe a child has given us data, email us and we will delete it.

Changes to this policy

If we make a material change to how we handle your data, we will update the date at the top of this page and tell you in the app. Where a change affects what you consented to, we will ask for your consent again.
