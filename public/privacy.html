TwoAgree — Privacy Policy

Last updated: [DATE — set at launch]

Who we are

TwoAgree is a faith-based couples compatibility app operated by [legal entity / individual name], contactable at [contact email]. For UK GDPR, the data controller is [controller name].

What we collect

  • Account data: email address, a display name, an optional short bio, and an optional profile photo.
  • Special-category data: your answers to questions about religious and philosophical beliefs, sex life, and health. These are treated as special-category data under UK GDPR Article 9 and are the core of what the app exists to surface.
  • Session data: the sessions you create or join, and your answers and predictions within them, shared only with the one partner in that session.

Why we collect it (lawful basis)

We process this data to provide the alignment features you ask for. For special-category data our lawful basis is your explicit consent (Article 9(2)(a)), given at sign-up. You can withdraw consent at any time by deleting your account. [Confirm lawful basis wording with a solicitor.]

Who can see your answers

Your answers in a session are visible to you and the single partner in that session, and only after you have both finished a level. They are not public and are not sold. We use Google Firebase (Authentication, Realtime Database, Hosting, Cloud Functions) as our data processor; infrastructure is hosted in the europe-west1 region.

Your rights

  • Access & portability: export all your data as JSON from Profile → “Export my data”.
  • Erasure: delete your account from Profile → “Delete my account”. This removes your profile and your contributions from every session (and deletes the session if you were the only member left).
  • Rectification: edit your name, bio, and photo any time in Profile.
  • You may also complain to the UK Information Commissioner’s Office (ICO).

Retention

We keep your data until you delete your account. [Define any additional retention limits / backups policy with a solicitor.]
